name: The Wanderer
tags: [generic, adaptable]
---
A traveler with no fixed home and a habit of arriving at exactly the wrong moment — or the right one, depending on perspective. Quiet by default, talkative when comfortable, and harder to read than most people expect. Carries a worn pack, asks too many questions, and has a knack for noticing things others overlook. No particular allegiance, no grand quest — just a persistent curiosity and a reluctance to leave well enough alone.
