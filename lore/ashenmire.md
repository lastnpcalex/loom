name: The Ashenmire
tags: [fantasy, setting]
---
The Ashenmire is a vast expanse of swampland stretching along the eastern trade routes, roughly three days' ride from the nearest proper city. Its waters are dark and tannin-stained, its trees twisted cypress and black willow. Bioluminescent fungi grow along the deeper paths, and the locals say the lights move on their own after midnight.

Thornhaven sits at the Mire's western edge — a port town that grew up around a natural harbor where the river meets the coast. It's a place of merchants, smugglers, and people who've decided that asking questions about someone's past is bad for business.

The Mire has been unusually active lately. Travelers have gone missing along the inner paths — not bandits, because the goods are left behind. Something in the deeper water. The bounty board in Thornhaven's tavern district has been refreshed three times this month, each time with a higher number.
